Headings and Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> - <h6>

    • <h1>Heading 1</h1>
    • <h2>heading 2</h2>
    • <h3>heading 3</h3>

  • Paragraph Tags: <p>

    • <p>This is my first paragraph</p>
    • <p>This is my second paragraph</p>

  • Sections: <div> and <span>

    • <div style="skyblue;">
      This is a div
      </div>
    • <span>This is a span</span><span>This is another span</span>
